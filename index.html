I am an associate professor at the Software Engineering Institute of ECNU, with the Shanghai Key Laboratory of Trustworthy Computing. I am leading the Intelligent Computing and Networking (ICN) Research Group. My research interests include Datacenter Networking, Machine Learning, AI-driven Intelligent Networking, SDN/NFV, Fog/Edge Computing and Cloud Computing. I obtained my Eng.Msc degree in Computer Infomation System Engineering from Warsaw Unviersity of Technology, Poland (sponsered by China Scholarship Council) in 2011 under the supervision of Prof. Jacek Wojciechowski, and my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from HKUST in 2015 under the supervision of Prof.Mounir Hamdi. Prior to joining ECNU in 2020, I worked at the Bell Labs as a research scientist from 2015 to 2016, and at Huawei as a senior engineer from 2016 to 2020.

I am looking for self-motivated students and visiting researchers. Please send me emails with your CV if you are interested in my projects and have relevant background (strong programming skills are required, AI/Machine Learning experiences are a plus).
